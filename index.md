## About Me

Hi! I am Lu Yang.
I am currently a Ph.D. student at the [Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), where I am fortunate to be supervised by [Prof. Longbo Huang](https://people.iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn/~huang/index.html). Prior to joining IIIS, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University in 2025.

## Research Interests
My research focuses on building intelligent agents capable of complex reasoning and adaptive decision-making. I am particularly interested in the intersection of **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** and **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**.
Currently, my research spans the following areas:
* **Meta-Reinforcement Learning**: Developing algorithms that enable agents to generalize across tasks and adapt rapidly to novel environments.
* **Multi-Agent Decision Making**: Exploring strategic coordination, competition, and communication in multi-agent systems.
* **Information Extraction & Representation**
